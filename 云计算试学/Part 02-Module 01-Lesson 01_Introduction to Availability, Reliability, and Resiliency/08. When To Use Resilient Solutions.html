08. When To Use Resilient Solutions

ND0063 C1 L0 08 When To Use Or Not Sure Resilient Solutions 1

Generally speaking, resilient systems are more expensive than non-resilient systems. They can also be more complex to run and maintain. Thus, you should consider the tradeoffs of where you do or do not need resiliency. Think through the use case and business requirements of a particular environment before deciding if you should be building out full redundancy.
