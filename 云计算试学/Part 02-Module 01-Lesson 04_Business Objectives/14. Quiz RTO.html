14. Quiz: RTO

RTO support

What is required to support a 2 hour RTO?
SOLUTION:
  • 24/7 support team
  • Remote access to systems
  • Documented recovery procedures

Calculate RTO

If your database goes down at 7:00pm and your application continues to run until 7:20pm but is failing to store the data it receives, and then the database is brought back online at 8:00pm, what was the RTO of the outage?
SOLUTION: 1 hour
