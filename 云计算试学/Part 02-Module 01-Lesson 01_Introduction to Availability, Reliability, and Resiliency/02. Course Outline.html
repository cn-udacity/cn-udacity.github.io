02. Course Outline

ND0063 C1 L0 03 Course Outline

Lessons

This course has the following lessons:

  • Regions and AZs
  • Building for Resiliency
  • Business Objectives
  • Monitoring, Reacting and Recovery

We will go through the components that allow us to achieve high availability in AWS and then discuss core AWS services and how to use them in a resilient manner. Then, we'll go through connecting business needs and requirements to uptime and availability goals and finish up with talking through how we keep an eye on the platforms we build and react to failures that occur.

Course Learning Objectives

You will be able to:

  • design high availability systems and networks that align to business availability objectives
  • implement high availability systems and networks according to their design
  • implement backup and recovery plans that meet business requirements
  • maintain a high availability platform in AWS through monitoring and alerting
